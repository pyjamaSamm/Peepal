About

Peepal is an up-and-coming startup based out of Bangalore, India. The objective of Peepal is to make smart innovations that can solve real world problems. Peepal was founded by 5 members in the year 2022.

Peepal has participated in various ideathons and competitions some of which are: the Aditya Birla Group Startup fulcrum, IIT Madras E-Summit, GLIM ideathon, and GDSC ideathon. Peepal has been extremely successful in all of the aforementioned events and has received high praise from investors and inventors. Peepal has been chosen for the final round of funding at the IIT Madras E-Summit, which will take place on April 8.
